Customer Segment Business Actions
=================================

1. Champions
-------------
- Provide exclusive early access to new products
- Offer loyalty rewards and VIP benefits
- Encourage referrals and reviews

2. Loyal Customers
-------------------
- Upsell premium or bundled products
- Offer subscription-based discounts
- Send personalized product recommendations

3. At Risk Customers
---------------------
- Send reactivation emails with discount offers
- Provide limited-time coupons
- Collect feedback to understand dissatisfaction

4. Regular Customers
---------------------
- Send engagement emails and promotions
- Offer bundle discounts
- Recommend trending or related products
